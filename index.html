About Us

Welcome to our website! We are a trading company that has been serving our customers since 2002. We offer a wide range of products, including all types of hand tools, power tools, hardware items, safety items, welding accessories, lifting materials, vibrator nozzles, electrical items and spares, and much more.

At our company, we are also proud to be authorized distributors for some of the top brands in the industry. These include Messer, Hikoki, Freemans, Hillson, and Arcon. We have built strong relationships with these brands, and we are committed to offering their high-quality products to our customers.

  • Messer is a global leader in the production and supply of industrial gases and gas cutting products
  • Hikoki (formerly known as Hitachi) is a well-known manufacturer of power tools and equipment.
  • Arcon is a leading manufacturer of welding and cutting equipment
  • Freemans(FMI Limited) is the largest manufacturer of measuring tapes, spirit levels and measuring wheels in the Indian sub-continent. they are a highly vertically integrated measuring tape manufacturing company, with state of the art in-house injection moulding and steel cold rolling facilities.
  • Hillson is a manufacturer of safety products, including safety shoes , gloves, and helmets. We believe that safety is a top priority in any workplace, and we are proud to offer Hillson's high-quality safety products to our customers.

As general order suppliers, we work with a variety of customers across a range of industries, from manufacturing to construction and more. We pride ourselves on offering high-quality products at competitive prices, with exceptional service to match.

We understand that each of our customers has unique needs, and we strive to provide personalized service to each and every one of them. Whether you're looking for a specific product or need help identifying the right solution for your business, our team is here to help.

Thank you for choosing us as your industrial and construction site supply partner.
